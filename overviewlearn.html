Industries

Get deep expertise where it matters: in your industry, and on your account.

The challenges business owners face in managing a successful enterprise vary from industry to industry. An issue that deeply impacts one business may have little effect on another. For this reason, it’s essential to work with Seattle accounting professionals who not only understand your business but also know your industry.

Accuracy's professionals have significant experience serving Seattle, Bellevue, Tacoma and Everett companies in specific industries such as dealerships, nonprofit, technology, real estate, and life sciences. Because our firm’s culture and clientele keep exceptional people here, you’ll find strong continuity and expertise on your account – specialists who ensure you’re in the best possible position for success. Partners are actively engaged on your client team to provide ongoing attention and advice.

History

Accuracy Accounting was founded in 2016 and since that time has grown into the largest CPA firm in the area. We are an assertive firm providing our clients a full range of services. Our offices are located in the Intermountain West because this is where we choose to live. However, we serve a wide variety of clients located throughout the United States. Because our firm is smaller than the national CPA firms, we maintain close and open relationships with each of our clients. Our commitment is to provide the highest quality services while retaining a small-town atmosphere. Accuracy Accounting is a member of the Private Companies Practice Section of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA) and is active in the local State Societies of CPAs.
